Why visit Guadalajara

Compared to Mexico City, Guadalajara is highly underestimated as the main tourist destination. Sure, some travelers pass by on their way to the Pacific coast and Sayulita's hippy hotspot, but it's often ignored without a second thought. However, it can be said that Guadalajara has as much to offer as the capital of Mexico and even has experiences and cultural heritage that you simply will not find in Mexico City. With that in mind, here are all the reasons why you should visit Guadalajara.
